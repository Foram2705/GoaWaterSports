All Goa Popular Watersports

Enjoy all Popular watersports combo included with this package.

Parasailing, Jet Ski Ride, Banana Ride, Bumper Ride, Speed Boat Ride.

Parasailing With Dip

Get Longest Parasailing in Goa with sea dip. Without sea dip parasailing is short so we give sea dip to all as complementary, while other people charge extra for it .

Banana Ride

One of the best Group Watersports in Goa.

in Banana Ride, There is a banana shaped boat which is connected to the speed boat which in turn pulls you. it is one of the exciting ride with group, the main aim is to keep balance of banana tube while it is moving with speed boat otherwise it will rotate and throw your group in the sea.

Bumper Ride

 In Bumper Ride, you will sit in a large air-filled tube dragged by a speed boat into the open clear sea. The main aim is to hang on as long as you can in this air-filled tube while the speed boat zips along the waves in order to throw you off in the sea. The rider gets the feel of a flight when the bumper boat bounces up.  it is a great opportunity for all the thrill-seekers.

Jetski Ride

Enjoy the jetski ride with our lifeguard at the centre of the sea.

Jet skis can go pretty fast, usually 40 mph to 70 mph, depending on how calm the water is, the amount of wind resistance, and the weight of the passengers. 

Speed Boat Ride

Experience thrilling speed boat ride in open sea. Feel the fresh air and waves of Arabian Sea.
